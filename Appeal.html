AN APPEAL

Our history of extending help to the real needy on a focused manner were successful since 1999. We are a group of like-minded professionals who are allotting part of our time to help the needy in our society. So far our focus were limited to certain areas therefore, we didn’t seek donations outside most of the times.

Moreover, we depend on various organizations for Cateract Surgery, Dental Screening, Hepatitis-B vaccination, Medical companies whom we collect medicine samples for Health Camps in the villages thereby we could minimize the overhead costs.

Today we have an identity in carrying out many programs - be it Education, or Health, or Eye Screening & Cateract, or HIV/AIDS Awareness programs, or taking care of an accident victim etc. We offer Allopathy, Homeo & Acupuncture methods of treatment in the villages, and schools in and around Chennai

Current projects, which needs funding assistance-

(1) To build a computer data centre for our adopted school - General Cariappa Secondary School. The requirement is for 10 PIV computers,UPS, AC, 10 work-stations, and a trained faculty.
(2) A Laptop with LCD projector for HIV/AIDS awareness programs
(3) A computer training centre with 10 computers facilitating to train-up qualified HIV/AIDS affected persons in BPO related activities.
(4) To adopt a village which needs our services, and elevate it to a Role Model village by improving their standard of life with basic amenities.
