MULTI SPECIALITY MEGA MEDICAL CAMP AT CAPTAIN MAHAL - TONDAIYARPET, CHENNAI ON 15th FEB 2020
IN COLLABORATION WITH
SHRI RAMACHNDRAN MEDICAL COLLEGE - PORUR, SANKARA EYE HOSPITAL - PAMMAL & TABLETS INDIA - TONDAIYARPET

Date: 15TH FEB 2020

Project: MULTI SPECIALITY MEGA MEDICAL CAMP

Venue: CAPTAIN MAHAL, TONDAIYARPET, CHENNAI

Camp held in Tondaiyarpet, Chennai in Association with Tablets India started at 8 am to 4 pm which was a great Success. EYE camp conducted by SANKARA EYE HOSPITAL PAMMAL AND all other facilities orgainized thorugh RAMACHANDRA MEDICAL COLLEGE - PORUR - CHENNAI. Following facilities organizaed in the camp

1. General Allopathy

2. Cardiology n ECG

3. Tuberculosys

4. Ortho - with automated machine checking of Bone Density

5. Diabetes Detection & treatment

6. Acupuncture and Homeo

7. Eye Screening & Contract Surgery

8. Dental Screening and Treatment

9. Gynaecologist

10. Paediatrician
