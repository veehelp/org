Free Cleft Lip surgery for children:
In association with rotary international, we provide free surgery to all children with "Cleft Lip" and have tied up arrangements with the following hospitals in Tamil Nadu.
1. CMC, Vellore
2. Dr. Jeyasekharan Centre for Cleft Care, Nagercoil
3. Meenakshi Mission Hospital & Research Centre, Coimbatore
4. Ganga Hospital, Madurai
5. Sri Ramchandra Medical College & Research Institute, Chennai
6. Vinayaka Mission Hospital, Salem
