About Founder:
  Sasikanth S , born in a middle class agricultural family. After his graduation, he served in the public sector for few years, and had the opportunity to travel across the country and got the opportunity of acquating various levels of people in the society. Later having attained some education in the computing line, he entered into the computing industry in the year 1988 and then rise to the position of a Computer Consultant in the industry. In addition to his background in management related areas, he is also a Cost Accountant and HR expert. Widely known as Sasi in the computing industry, his contributions are significant in the field of Human Resource Development globally.

He served in the Lions Club International over a decade and adored various positions, including Millennium Charter President in one of the clubs, Lions Club of Chennai Capital. He adopted few villages in and around Chennai as part of his visionary ideas to spread education in the villages, and also offer medical aid to the real needy. His major focus in the adopted villages were to promote Awareness on balanced and natural life and organized programs in association with other NGOs.

His Lionistic awareness gave birth to HELP movement, a permanent solution for effective projects in a more meaningful manner. He intends to spread HELP as a movement in the society as part of everybody’s life and build a platform for all of us to do our bit. It do not attract any investment, in most of the cases, but only willingness!

His strong contention is to spread the facilities of education as the birth right of each and every child in our society. He believes, rather offering material benefits to somebody, the offer should be on awareness which would help a human to have a balanced and Self-less life, and he/she would become a perfect human by himself/herself. This would, by default, help them to attain their Goal of Life!
