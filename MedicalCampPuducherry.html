MULTI SPECIALITY MEGA MEDICAL CAMP IN PUDUCHERRY ON 21ST SEP 2019

Date: 21ST SEPTEMBER 2019

Project: MULTI SPECIALITY MEGA MEDICAL CAMP

Venue: ALLIANZ BIOSCIENCES PVT LTD, THIRUVANDAR KOVIL, THIRUBUVANAI, PUDUCHERRY

EYE & CATERACT SURGERY

Aravind Eye Hospital, Puducherry

To inspect the site arrangements, Representative from above Hospital by name Mr. Thiruvengadam visited the site in advance. As advised, arranged the following:

a) Registration desk

b) Vision test desk

c) Doctors Desk

d) Refraction

e) Spectacles desk

f) Additional desk

It had been decided to offer free spectacles to the shortlisted persons.

24 persons were shortlisted for cateract surgery

110 persons were offered FREE spectacles

Total 271 persons were screened, and treated with medicine.

Project: MULTI SPECIALITY HEALTH CHECKUP

In Alliance with MAHATMA GANDHI MEDICAL & RESEARCH INSTITUTE - PUDUCHERRY

To inspect the site arrangements, Representative from above Hospital by name Mr. Tegesh visited the site.

They brought following medical team:

(a) General Medicines – 4doctors

(b) General Surgery – 2 doctors

(c) Pediatrician - 2 doctors

(d) ENT -2 doctors

(e) Orthopedics – 2 doctors

(f) Registration desk

(g) Nurses – 3 Numbers

(h) Admin executive to supervise & coordinate the events

(i) ECG, Sugar Test, Weight machine and BP apparatus they brought with them.

The team consisted of 20 members, who actively involved in the functions.

They also brought medicines worth about Rs. 70000/- to 75000.

Total patients attended for General health screening 410

Diabetic screening done for 52 patients

ECG taken for 8 patients

For Dental separate mobile van was arranged to carry out –

(i) General cleaning

(ii) Simple extractions

(iii) Scaling

(iv) Filling

Over 50 patients were screened and treated in Dental.

Food: Breakfast and Lunch arranged for the medical team and camp volunteers, including refreshments.

Publicity: Auto publicity carried out for 3 days, in addition to door to door visit by volunteers to brief about the event. Also given Ad in the local newspapers.

Local MLA with her family visited the camp site.

Temporary toilet: Three numbers of temporary toilets were arranged and will be fixed one day in advance

The camp was a major success, with everybodys participation. We have offered a Certificate of Appreciation with a Gift to all Health team, including Doctors and Volunteers

We also take this opportunity to thank one and all
