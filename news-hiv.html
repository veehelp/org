MEDICAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM:

HIV/AIDS:   In order to eradicate the menace of HIV/AIDS,  our firm belief is that to organize Awareness Programs amongst various levels of people in the society.  HELP Movement volunteers are involved in promoting Awareness programs on HIV/AIDS in the society, especially in the villages. AIDS awareness camps are organized in association with Tamil Nadu AIDS Control Board and we bring learned speakers who can deliver valuable information about HIV/AIDS so as to keep precautions. We also circulate pamphlets and informative articles on AIDS in the society. We are also organizing free medicines for the AIDS affected poor patients. Today, most of the HIV/AIDS affected patients are not willing to come forward for treatment with the fear of their social status.  HELP Movement recognizes such situation, and would like to deliver free medicines in a confidential manner, based on need. 

Funeral assistance: We undertake funeral program for HIV/AIDS patients.  For more details, pls get in touch with the trust volunteers.

Health Camps:  We organize Health camps in the rural areas so as to help the (i) Old-aged, and (2) Pregnant women and (3) Children.  We also organize nutrition to pregnant women.  Our camps are well organized with adequate and continued supply of medicines for the needy.  General Health camps cover the following –

(i)   EyeCare including Cataract surgery

(ii)  DentalCARE, including filling, extraction etc

(iii) General health Check-up, including ChildCARE

Awareness Programs:  As we rightly mentioned elsewhere in the site, our  philosophy is not to offer a fish to a hungry person, but to make him Fishing!  Yes, we are successful in organizing various Awareness Programs, including Literacy, Sanitation, Health etc.  We also would like to promote the Role of Spirituality  in Common Man.
