EYE CARE
  • Sight-First is one of our focused areas, as we value the sight.  The importance of sight will be known once we lost it.  Therefore, HELP Movement initiates program to identify such poor old-aged persons, and would organize Cataract at a specified time.  We accept registrations of such needy persons, who will be intimated the date and time for such surgery once we could accumulate 15-20 persons.  We are dependable  for such treatment with other like-minded NGOs, like Lions Club International etc.

  • We organize eye screening camps in various villages with periodic intervals. The short listed persons are taken for cataract surgery from the camp site itself. 

  • Once the surgery is over, the patients are dropped at their homes, with required medicines and also guidance to take care of their eyes for another 45-60 days. They are again required to visit the hospital for periodic eye check ups, which they will have to take care themselves.

  • We also help in promoting awareness programs on eye donation.
