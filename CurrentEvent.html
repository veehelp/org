MEGA MEDICAL CAMP AT CHENNAI

Date: 24 August 2019

To commomerate the Founders Day (AMMAJI DAY), a mega medical camp is arranged in association with TABLETS INDIA LIMITED, CHENNAI, as per details below:

Venue: Captain Mahal Marriage Hall, Tondiarpet High Road (OppAppollo Hospital) Chennai

Allopathic treatment:

A Group of medical practioners of various disciplines will manage the Allopathy division. Based on prescription, free medicines will be distributed.

Eye treatment- SankaraEye Hospital, Pammal, Chennai

Free Eye screening will be done to shortlist patients for Cateract surgery at Sankara Eye Hospital. All shortlisted patients being transported same day to Sankara Eye Hospital to conduct all required tests before surgery. Surgery will be performed in the next day. For the major treatment required patients, arrangements will be made at Sankara Eye Hospital. Spectacles: Eligible persons will be provided with free spectacles

Dental treatment- Ragaas Dental & Medical College, Uthandi, Perungudi, Chennai

Will be conducting the Dental treatment of general cleaning, extraction etc. Major treatment required persons will be taken to the Medical college for admission.

apart from above following treatment also avaialble in the camp

1. Diabetic Detection

2. Accupuncture/Accu Pressure

3. Homeo Treatment

4. Detection of Tuberculosis

5. ECG facility

6. Arthritis treatment

Aravind started in 1976 as an 11-bed eye clinic in an old temple-city. Today it is the largest and most productive eye care facility in the world. Taking its compassionate services to the doorstep of rural India, Aravind's stunningly effective strategies vaulted barriers of distance, poverty and ignorance to create a self-sustaining system that now treats over 1.7 million patients each year, two-thirds of them, for free.
