K.K Bernard - Board of Trustee

A born philanthropist, who has already seen his life across the world.

A Graduate by education, he was working with the Ministry of Finance for over 25 years in Delhi.
He and his wife (late) were very helpful to the poor people, whom they have organized many projects, including marriage assistance, employment assistance. His house being a common place to visit for help even at midnight.

His vision is to help the poor and street children, and bring them up in their life thru proper education.He is very instrumental to start a Childcare center where 10-15 children can be accommodated and provide tem proper life to settle down in life.

During his Delhi days, he contributed remarkably to bring up a place of worship in R.K.Puram-Delhi.Later this project became so successful, and he could bring people from overseas who could bring it up further growth.
