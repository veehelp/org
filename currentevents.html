BLOOD DONATION CAMP IN CHENNAI & PUDUCHERRY ON 23.08.2019

Project No.1

Date: 23rd AUGUST 2019

Project: Blood Donation Camp

Venue: Thiuvandarkoil, Whirlpool Road, Puducherry 605 102

Project: Blood donation camp

Venue: #179, Tondiarpet High road, Chennai 600 081

75TH BIRTHDAY CELEBRATION ON 19TH JULY 2019

75th BIRTHDAY CELEBRATION

Date : 19th July 2019

Venue: GUNIAGRAHARA, BANGALORE

vHELP Social Foundation is always there to honour Educational Institutions and thereby Teachers in particular. We did adopt several schools in Chennai and its suburbs to implement our awareness programs, and also offer a helping hand to their infrastructure need. Cariappa Secondary School and Cauvery school etc are some of the model for this in Chennai.

We got an opportunity to honour a Retired Teacher in Bangalore, whose 75th Birthday celebrations we have done in a grand manner.

Shri Ramachandran master, resident of Kalathammanahalli village in Bangalore North was the hero. We have organized the function in Sidheswara temple in Guni Agrahara village on 19th July 2019. We have invited his village people to participate, and get blessings. 7 homams were arranged when the function started at 5.30am, went on till 4pm. The entire family of the teacher participated, along with part of his village people in Kalathammanahalli. Total around 100 people participated, for whom Breakfast and Lunch were arranged.
