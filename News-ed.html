SIGHT FIRST:

 

EyeCARE:  One of our main programs which gives a focused approach to provide sight those who lack the same.  We organize community camps frequently, especially in villages, and test the eyes of the needy.

 

Shortlisted  persons are classified for different types of treatment, including Eye treatment, Supply of spectacles and Surgery.

 

Cataract Surgery:  This is our main program in the villages.  Our aim is to identify sightless people anywhere in the society, and examine their eyes and incase found cataract, we organise free cataract surgery, with the very latest technology methods.  Our volunteers take care of the complete need of the patient till he is discharged and dropped at his/her home.

 

Eye Donation:  In association with Lions Club International, we organize eye donations from those who are interested.  Interested parties may get in touch with the Trust volunteers for registration.
