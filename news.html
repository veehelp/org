The Trust would like to extend financial HELP to economically poor children, those who are good in their studies. Our interest is to identify the most economically weaker families without the feeling of caste, colour, creed or religion, where the parents are sick, jobless, unable to meet their children educational expenses. The Trust would extend help primarily for education upto high school level. However, higher studies will be looked into in a case to case basis. The child should have good academic record with proper behavioural manners.

The Trust would examine the background of the family and would extend support for those who really deserved such assistance. The Trust own the responsibility either to grant or refuse any such applications, and no demand, in this connection is being entertained at whatsoever nature.

Interested children or their parents are requested to get in touch with the Trust officials, in this connection.

Eye Donation:
The Trust acted swiftly when the mother of one of our Volunteers passed away during the month of February 2004 at a village nearby Chennai city. The process was supported by the Lions Club International.

Both the eyes are donated. The entire initiative was taken by Mr. Ravichandran timely, who is a Computer professional. He is associated with HELP Movement activities for the past so many years.


Organising awareness programs on HIV/AIDS:
HELP Movement volunteers are involved in promoting Awareness programs on HIV/AIDS in the society, especially in the villages. AIDS awareness camps are organized in association with Tamil Nadu AIDS Control Board and we bring learned speakers who can deliver valuable information about HIV/AIDS so as to keep precautions. We also circulate pamphlets and informative articles on AIDS in the society.

We are also organizing free medicines for the AIDS affected poor patients. Today, most of the HIV/AIDS affected patients are not willing to come forward for treatment with the fear of their social status. HELP Movement recognizes such situation, and would like to deliver free medicines in a confidential manner, based on need. We also would like to courier such medicines, provided the patients are registered with us.

We therefore, request HIV/AIDS affected patients to register with us for such benefits. We confirm, such details will not be disclosed to anybody.
We also would intend to offer funeral assistance for HIV/AIDS patients. Please get in touch with the Trust officials to do the needful


CATARACT for poor villagers:
Sight-First is one of our focused areas, as we value the sight. The importance of sight will be known once we lost it. Therefore, HELP Movement initiates program to identify such poor old-aged persons, and would organize Cataract at a specified time. We accept registrations of such needy persons, who will be intimated the date and time for such surgery once we could accumulate 15-20 persons. We are dependable for such treatment with other like-minded NGOs, like Lions Club International etc.
