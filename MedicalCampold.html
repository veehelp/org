MULTI SPECIALITY MEGA MEDICAL CAMP IN CHENNAI ON 24TH AUG 2019

Date: 24TH AUGUST 2019

Project: MULTI SPECIALITY MEGA MEDICAL CAMP

Venue: CAPTAIN MAHAL, TONDAIYARPET, CHENNAI

Camp held in Tondaiyarpet, Chennai in Association with Tablets India started at 8 am to 4 pm which was a great Success. Organized following

1. General Allopathy

2. Cardiology n ECG

3. Tuberculosys

4. Ortho - with automated machine checking of Bone Density

5. Diabetes Detection & treatment

6. Acupuncture and Homeo

7. Eye Screening & Contract Surgery

8. Dental Screening and Treatment

9. Gynaecologist

10. Paediatrician

* Contract : 650 patients had been screened, of which 20 patients are taken for catract surgery First Batch. Over 200 spectacles were screen and the same will be distributed on 5th Sep 2019

* More than 75 patients screened in Dental and 40 patients were treated

* More than 800 patients attended for General medical treatment and issued free medicines, tonic etc in the camp

Thanks to Tablets India management who sponsered the event and the volunteers who helped a lot to make grand success of this program. Tablets India sponsored mediciens for the prescribed patients including children, women and general patients. ECG shortlisted patients had been advised for further treatment in a hospital.
