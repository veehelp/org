BLOOD DONATION CAMP AT PUDUCHERRY & CHENNAI

Project No.1

Date: 23rd AUGUST 2019

Project: Blood Donation Camp

Venue: Thiuvandarkoil, Whirlpool Road, Puducherry 605 102

A Total of 63 donors participated in the camp, and donated blood. JIPMER arranged the blood collection thru a MobileVan from 9.30am to 1.30pm thru their dedicated volunteers.

The event was sponsored by Allianz Biosciences, Puducherry on their Founders 46th Anniversary. JIPMER issued Donor Certificates to all the 63 donors, and gifts by Allianz Biosciences. The camp was a grand success.

Project No.2

Date: 23rd AUGUST 2019

Project: Blood donation camp

Venue: #179, Tondiarpet High road, Chennai 600 081

A total of 28 donors participated in the camp, and donated blood. Lions Club Blood Bank thru their Mobile van participated in the event, and collected the blood in the morning till 1.30pm.

The event was sponsored by Tablets India Ltd, Chennai on their Founder Shri R.S.Jhaver (Babuji) 46th Anniversary Day. Donor certificates were issued to all Donors, and gifts by Tablets India. The camp was a great success.
